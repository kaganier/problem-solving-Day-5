Day Care Dilemma

Problem

Jenny owns a daycare. the kids or scheduled for a 30 minute recess. She has 10 kids in her class During recess they are offered 2 options for activities they can participate in. Option 1 is hopscotch and option 2 is swinging on the swing set. How many will choose option 2 as their activity for recess?

Solution

let class be the kids in the class
            For: each kid in the class

            IF: the kid chooses option 2
             THEN: Tally
             ELSE: Do not tally

Result

Line the kids up in a row. Go to each kid and, ask which activity do they choose to participate in today? For each kid that chooses option 2 add one tally mark to your log.
